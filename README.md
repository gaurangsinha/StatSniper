Stat Sniper is a fun and educational game that trains your brain to intuitively understand data distributions. Master the concepts of mean, median, and percentiles to make smarter, faster judgments about data.

1.  **Open `index.html`:** Open the `index.html` file in your web browser to start the game.
2.  **Analyze the Chart:** Each round, you'll see a chart with a distribution of data points.
3.  **Make Your Guess:** Your objective will be displayed (e.g., "Find Mean"). Click on the chart to place your guess for that statistical measure.
4.  **Adjust Your Guess:** You can click and drag your guess to fine-tune its position.
5.  **Submit and Learn:** Once you've placed all your guesses, click "Submit Guess". The game will show you the actual values and give you a score based on your accuracy.
6.  **Continue to the Next Round:** Click "Next Round" to continue honing your skills!
